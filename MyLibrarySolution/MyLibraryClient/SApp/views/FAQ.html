FAQs

Are all products on TechShop original and genuine?

Yes. We are committed to offering our customers only 100% genuine and original products. We also take all necessary actions to ensure this: any seller found to be selling non-genuine products is immediately delisted from TechShop. Please send an email to customer.care@TechShop.com.bd if you think a product listed on our website does not meet these standards.

How do I place an order?

Shopping on TechShop is easy! Once you have found the product you want to buy, just follow the steps below:

  • Click ’buy now’, to add this product to your cart,
  • Click ’Go to next step’ in the top right corner,
  • You will then need to fill in your contact details and preferred shipping address,
  • Chose your preferred payment option before clicking the ’Confirm order’ button.

How do I pay on TechShop?

We have the following payment methods through which you can make a payment:

  • Cash On Delivery (easy and simple at your doorstep)
  • Credit/Debit Card

How can I get a refund or replacement for my product?

Step 1: Start the Easy & Fast Return

  • Login to your account at www.GsShop.com.bd , click "My Orders", "Orders Details" and "Return" and follow the instructions on the screen! OR
  • Call us at 16492 to create your return, select the product you wish to return on the returns form that arrived with your order. Include this form in your package

Step 2: Pack your product

  • Pack your product, including all tags and accessories

Step 3: Return your product

  • If you requested a pickup, we will contact you to arrange a pickup time within 2 days OR
  • If you selected a drop-off: Inside-Dhaka: Drop your item at 66/4 West Rajabazar, Tejgaon, Dhaka-1215 Outside-Dhaka: Drop your item at any Sundarban Courier service hub, addressed to Mr.Alamgir, C/O-Daraz, Sundarban Courier Service, Kamarpara, Dhaka

Step 4: What's next

  • We will send a tracking number via SMS after the package has been picked up or dropped off. We will immediately initiate your replacement after it passes our quality evaluation. For expected timielines see page 2 of this form

Are there any hidden costs or charges if I order from TechShop?

There are no hidden costs or charges when you order from TechShop. All costs are 100% visible at the end of the checkout process.

How can I track my order?

We will send you regular updates about the status of your order via emails and SMS. After your order has left our warehouse and is on its way to you, you can also track its status by entering your tracking number on RPS Library
